Welcome to the Engineering Team Handbook. This document covers our development practices, code review standards, on-call rotation policies, and team norms. All new engineers should read this during their first week and discuss any questions with their onboarding buddy. We follow trunk-based development with short-lived feature branches. All pull requests require at least one approval before merging. On-call rotations are weekly and shared across the full-stack team. Incidents are triaged using a severity scale from P0 to P3.
