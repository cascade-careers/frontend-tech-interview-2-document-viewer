New hire onboarding checklist — Day 1: Complete I-9 verification, set up laptop and accounts, meet your manager and buddy. Week 1: Attend orientation sessions, review team handbook, complete compliance training. Week 2: Shadow team members, set up development environment, submit first PR. Week 3: Take on a starter task from the backlog and participate in sprint ceremonies. Week 4: Complete onboarding survey and schedule a 30-day check-in with your manager.
